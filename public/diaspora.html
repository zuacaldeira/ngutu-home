NGUTU DIASPORA

NGUTU DIASPORA aims at Effective Socio-Economic Emancipation of the African Diaspora. We will promote actions to help building a global network of products and services from and to africans, that will help our community to organize towards creation of jobs, thus reducing unemployment among african emigrants.

We are also committed to engage in fair access to justice and social inclusion, by fighting against social and state descrimination of people of African Descent.
